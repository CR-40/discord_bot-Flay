Flay Bot Privacy Policy

How Flay Bot collects, uses, and protects data

1. Information Collected

Flay Bot may collect and store the following information when used: server IDs, user IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and bot configuration settings. This data is required for the bot to function properly.

2. How Information is Used

The collected information is used only to provide bot functionality, such as saving settings, responding to commands, and improving features. Data is not used for advertising or tracking.

3. Data Sharing

Flay Bot does not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. Data is only used internally for bot operation.

4. Data Storage and Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data. However, no system is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

5. Data Removal

Server owners may remove Flay Bot from their server at any time, which stops further data collection. You may also request data deletion by contacting the developer.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept the updated policy.

7. Contact

For questions or data requests, contact the developer via Discord or the official support server.
